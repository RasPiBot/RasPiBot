About

An open source Robotics platform accessible to beginners and experts.
Learn skills in Coding, Electronics, Linux, Networking and 3D Printing.

Low cost

We will teach you how to build your own humanoid robot for under $200.

Raspberry Pi

The Raspberry Pi controller is a complete computer. Your robot has access to Internet.

3D printable chassis

All models of robots are open source and can be made with a 3D printer.

Robots' controler is a Rasberry Pi combined with an Adafruit 16 Channel Servo Driver.

Raspberry Pi controller
  • 1.2GHz 64-bit quad-core ARMv8 CPU
  • 1GB RAM
  • 40 GPIO pins
  • Full HDMI port
  • 802.11n Wireless LAN
  • Bluetooth 4.1
  • Fast-Ethernet port
  • Micro SD card slot
Adafruit 16 Channel Servo Driver
  • Drive up to 16 servomotors/sensors
  • Stack another one to control 16 more

All our robots are printable

Mission

We believe that every kid should be able to afford a humanoid robot. RasPiBot offers affordable kits with an open source library and tutorials. Unleash the power of the Raspberry Pi and start coding your dream robot.

Open source library

Our free Python library works with any developers tools.

Open source 3D models

Print your own robot shell.

Affordable parts

We found the cheapest servomotors/sensors so that anyone can jump into robotic.

Touch screen

Control your robot with a 3 inche touch screen on his chest.

Camera

Enable face recognition with our high definition camera.

Share with community

You can share your creations with our community.

Wireless

Control your robot over SSH protocol from a distant computer.

Bluetooth

Jump into domotic at home. Your robot can even control your smart home.

4 USB ports

Connect any USB device to your robot.
